Why Stainless Steel Bollards Are Essential for Modern Urban Spaces
5 Key Benefits
Superior Corrosion Resistance SS 304 and SS 316 grades provide excellent protection against rust, especially in coastal, high-humidity and polluted environments.
High Impact Strength & Safety Sturdy construction helps control vehicle movement, protect pedestrians, buildings and infrastructure from accidental or deliberate impacts.
Aesthetic & Modern Appeal Sleek, polished or brushed stainless steel finish enhances the visual appeal of streets, parks, plazas and commercial areas.
Low Maintenance & Long Life Weather-resistant and easy to clean — requires almost no upkeep compared to mild steel or painted bollards.
Versatile Applications Ideal for traffic management, parking demarcation, pedestrian zones, landscaping, smart cities and high-security areas.

Materials & Types of SS Bollards
Material Options
SS 304 – Excellent corrosion resistance for general urban and inland applications
SS 316 – Marine-grade stainless steel for coastal, high-humidity and aggressive environments
Types of SS Bollards
Fixed Bollards – Permanent installation for traffic control and boundary definition
Removable Bollards – Flexible access control with locking mechanism
Decorative Bollards – Sleek designs for aesthetic street furniture and landscaping
Surface-Mounted / Baseplate Bollards – Easy installation on existing floors
Reflective Bollards – With high-visibility reflective strips for better night safety
