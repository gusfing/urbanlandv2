Section 2: Our Range of Benches
H2 Heading
Our Range of Premium Benches
Urbanland manufactures multiple types of high-quality benches to suit different environments, budgets, and design requirements:
WPC Benches
Wood-Plastic Composite benches offer the natural look of wood with superior weather resistance and zero maintenance. Ideal for gardens, balconies, and premium residential projects.
NFC Wood Benches
Natural Fiber Composite (NFC) benches deliver enhanced strength, a premium matte finish, and excellent weather resistance. Perfect for architectural projects, high-end public spaces, and modern landscapes.
Aluminium Benches
Lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and modern-looking. Perfect for coastal areas, rooftops, and contemporary public spaces.
Mild Steel Benches
Heavy-duty powder-coated mild steel benches provide excellent strength and cost-effectiveness. Ideal for high-traffic parks, schools, hospitals, and commercial complexes.
Stainless Steel Benches
Heavy-duty stainless steel benches deliver ultimate durability and a sleek, high-end finish for luxury developments and public spaces where long life and low maintenance are critical.
