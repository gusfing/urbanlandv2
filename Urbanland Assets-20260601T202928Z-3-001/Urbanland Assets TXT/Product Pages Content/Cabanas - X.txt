Frequently Asked Questions
Which material is best for poolside cabanas?
For poolside cabanas, WPC/tensile fabric and aluminium are often preferred because they handle moisture, splashes, and sun exposure very well. Stainless steel is also an excellent choice for premium, long-term performance, especially in high-humidity or coastal-style environments.
Can cabanas be customized in size and design?
Yes. All Urbanland cabanas can be customized in size, height, roof style, and overall design. You can choose from single, double, or larger multi-person cabanas, along with options like fixed, cantilever, or modular structures to match your site layout and design vision.
Are they suitable for coastal areas?
Yes. For coastal locations, we recommend using stainless steel or high-grade aluminium structures with appropriate coatings. These materials offer excellent corrosion resistance and longer lifespan in high-salinity, high-humidity environments typically found near the coast.
What is the typical lead time?
Typical lead time depends on the size and complexity of the project, but most cabana orders are manufactured and ready for installation within 3–6 weeks after final approval of design and specifications. Your exact schedule will be confirmed at the time of order.
Do you provide installation services?
Yes. We provide complete installation support across India through our in-house team and trained partners. Our team ensures proper foundation, alignment, fixing, and finishing so that your cabanas are safe, stable, and ready for long-term outdoor use.
