All Your Questions Answered
1. General Questions
What is Urbanland Products?
Urbanland Products is a leading Made-in-India manufacturer of premium, sustainable outdoor furniture including benches, planter boxes, bus shelters, dustbins, wicker furniture and more. We specialize in durable, low-maintenance solutions for real estate, hospitality, healthcare, education and municipal & smart city projects.
Where are your products manufactured?
All our products are proudly manufactured in Vasai, Maharashtra, India.
Are your products suitable for Indian climate?
Yes. Every product is engineered to withstand extreme heat, heavy monsoons, UV exposure and high usage.
2. Products & Materials
What materials do you use?
We primarily use WPC (Wood-Plastic Composite), NFC Wood (Natural Fiber Composite), Aluminium, Mild Steel (powder-coated) and Stainless Steel. We have completely moved away from traditional teak, sesam and acacia wood to promote sustainability.
What is the difference between WPC and NFC Wood?
WPC offers a natural wood look with excellent weather resistance. NFC Wood provides higher strength, a premium matte finish and longer lifespan. Both are highly sustainable and zero-maintenance alternatives to real wood.
Which material is best for coastal areas?
Aluminium and Stainless Steel are ideal for coastal and high-rainfall areas due to superior corrosion resistance.
Are your benches and planters suitable for high-traffic public areas?
Yes. Our Mild Steel, Stainless Steel and heavy-duty WPC/NFC products are specifically designed for 24/7 public use and vandalism-prone zones.
3. Sustainability & Green Products
How sustainable are your products?
Very. WPC and NFC Wood are made from recycled plastics and natural fibers, significantly reducing deforestation and carbon footprint. Our long-life products (12–20+ years) minimize replacement waste and strongly support IGBC, GRIHA and LEED green building certifications.
Do your products help with green building certifications?
Yes. Architects and developers frequently use our WPC, NFC Wood and recyclable metal products to earn points under IGBC, GRIHA and LEED standards.
4. Projects & Delivery

Most projects are completed in 2–4 weeks from design approval to installation.

Yes. We provide pan-India delivery and professional installation support for all projects.
Can you handle large-scale developer and municipal projects?
Absolutely. We have successfully delivered 50+ projects including large townships, smart cities and luxury real estate developments.
5. Warranty & Support
What is your warranty coverage?
All products come with India’s only 2-Year Comprehensive Warranty covering frame, seating surface, powder coating and manufacturing defects.
Do you provide after-sales support?
Yes. We offer dedicated after-sales support and guidance even after project completion.
6. Customization & Quotations
Can products be customised?
Yes. We offer full customisation in size, shape, colour, finish, branding and design to match your project requirements.

H2 Heading
Why Material Selection is Critical for Outdoor Furniture
In India’s diverse climate, the right material determines longevity, maintenance cost, aesthetics and environmental impact. Traditional wood (teak, sesam, acacia) is increasingly replaced by modern, sustainable alternatives like WPC and NFC Wood because they offer superior performance while supporting green building certifications (IGBC, GRIHA, LEED).

Section 3: Detailed Material Breakdown
H2 Heading
Understanding Each Material
WPC (Wood-Plastic Composite)
Made from recycled plastics and wood fibers. Offers the warm look of wood with zero maintenance and excellent weather resistance. Highly sustainable — reduces deforestation and supports a circular economy.
NFC Wood (Natural Fiber Composite)
Advanced natural fiber composite with superior strength and premium matte finish. Longer lifespan than WPC, ideal for architectural and large-scale landscaping projects.
Aluminium
Lightweight, corrosion-resistant and modern. Perfect for coastal and rooftop applications where weight and rust resistance are critical.
Mild Steel (Powder Coated)
Cost-effective and extremely strong. Best for high-traffic public areas, municipal parks and general-use applications.
Stainless Steel
Premium, ultra-durable and virtually maintenance-free. Ideal for luxury developments and high-exposure coastal environments.
