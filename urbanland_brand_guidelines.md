## Step 3 — Brand Personality & Tone of Voice

### Brand Positioning Statement
> Urbanland Products is India's premium outdoor furniture brand, crafting nature-inspired, globally competitive spaces for corporates, hotels, and hospitality businesses who refuse to compromise on quality.

### Tone of Voice

**Quality** • **Bold** • **Confident** • **Inspiring** • **Direct**

**What it means in practice:**
- Make strong statements. No weak language like "we try" or "we hope"
- Speak like an expert. Own your space in the market
- Paint a picture of beautiful outdoor spaces
- No fluff. Every word earns its place

### Words Urbanland Uses
**Expert · Premium · Crafted · Durable · Transforming · Built to last · Nature-inspired · Precision · Global · Trusted**

### Words Urbanland Avoids
**Cheap · Affordable · Try · Hope · Basic · Simple · Budget**

### Brand Voice Examples

**WEAK — Avoid**  
"We try to provide good quality furniture at reasonable prices."

**STRONG — Use**  
"We craft outdoor spaces that endure seasons, impress clients, and stand the test of time."

### Typography

| Role           | Style                          | Feel |
|----------------|--------------------------------|------|
| **Headline Font**  | Serif (e.g. Playfair Display)     | Authoritative, premium, timeless |
| **Body Font**      | Clean Sans-serif (e.g. Inter)     | Modern, readable, confident |
| **Accent Font**    | Italic Serif                      | Creative, nature-inspired touches |

### Photography & Imagery Direction

**USE:**
- Wide, aspirational outdoor spaces — hotels, resorts, corporate campuses
- Close-up shots of craftsmanship, texture, material quality
- Natural light, golden hour photography
- Real installation project photos

**AVOID:**
- White studio product shots
- Stock imagery that feels generic
- Cluttered or busy backgrounds
